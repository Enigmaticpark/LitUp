Too lazy to check all your event invites? Want to see what your friends are up to on the weekend? Get lit.

 

LitUp enables users to see which events their friends are going to, and how lit it's going to be. Using data from Facebook, LitUp can show you the best events in your area, even one's your friends are not going to as long as it's lit.

 

Based out of McGill University in Montreal, Quebec, founders Thomas and Edward have joined forces to create LitUp.
